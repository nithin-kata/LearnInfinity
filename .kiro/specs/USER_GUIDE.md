### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Platform**: Navigate to the LearnInfinity homepage
2. **Click "Get Started"**: Find this button in the sidebar or hero section
3. **Fill Registration Form**:
   - **Name**: Your full name (minimum 2 characters)
   - **Email**: Valid email address (will be your login username)
   - **Password**: Secure password (minimum 6 characters)
4. **Welcome Bonus**: Receive 24 free credits upon successful registration!

### First Login

After registration, you'll be automatically logged in. For future visits:
1. Click "Get Started" or navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login" to access your dashboard

## üí≥ Understanding the Credit System

### What Are Credits?
Credits are the currency of LearnInfinity. Instead of paying money, you use credits to access learning content and instructor sessions.

### Credit Economics
- **Starting Balance**: 24 free credits for new users
- **Learning Cost**: 1 credit per hour of platform usage
- **Earning Credits**: Teach others to earn credits
- **Automatic Deduction**: Credits are deducted automatically after each hour of activity

### Managing Your Credits
- **View Balance**: Check your current credits in the sidebar dashboard
- **Track Usage**: Monitor your learning hours in your profile
- **Earn More**: Offer your skills to other users to earn additional credits
